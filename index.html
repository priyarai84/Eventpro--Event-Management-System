About EventPro

Greater Noida & Delhi NCR's trusted event management company — crafting unforgettable experiences since March 2026.

Our Story

EventPro was founded in March 2026 by Priya Rai with a clear vision — to make every event in Greater Noida and Delhi NCR truly special, regardless of budget or size.


We started with a simple belief: every occasion — whether a birthday, wedding, corporate event, or a small gathering — deserves professional planning and flawless execution. That belief drives everything we do.


What we do: We handle end-to-end event management — from initial concept and decoration planning to vendor coordination, on-site management, and post-event cleanup. You enjoy the moment; we handle everything else.


Our services cover Birthday Party Planning, Wedding Ceremonies, Corporate Events, and Social Gatherings like anniversaries, baby showers, farewells, and kitty parties — all across Greater Noida and Delhi NCR.
